-- Esper by Jumpei --

When I was just a kid, I remember drawing a small sequencial comic (and by small, I really do mean it) in a corner of a piece of paper, the character was a bit of a much more basic version of the Prince of Persia guy and the guy from Another World(*). Besides those inspirations I also was very much inspired by the Fighting Fantasy books, so I thought this little character could have been the protagonist of some adventures of his own. I have to find those early sketches but with time the idea got to be slightly more developed as some examples I show here.
